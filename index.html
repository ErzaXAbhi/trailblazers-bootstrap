About Us

RIDING BIKES AND BUILDING TRAILS

Trailblazers is the friendly, family mountain biking club. We are a passionate group of mountain bike enthusiasts who came together during the challenging period of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Our love for the sport knows no bounds. We find joy and exhilaration in exploring the rugged terrains and scenic landscapes that surround us. Whether it's tackling challenging off-road trails or cruising along smooth roads, we thrive on the thrill of the ride and the beauty of the journey.
